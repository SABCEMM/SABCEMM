◆ updateTradingVolume()

void AgentHarras::updateTradingVolume ( const double &  newPrice)
protected

Updates the decision and the tradingVolume of the agent.

Depends on the opinion of the agent. If the opinion is greater then the agent's threshold, he buys stock –> decision +1 If the opinion is smaller then minus the agent's threshold, he sells stock –> decision -1 In any other case the agent remains passive –> decision 0 The agent is only allowed to buy/sell with the percentage g of his cash/stock.
